In the interest of comprehensive, it is worth mentioning an episode reported in some texts of vertebral column anatomopathology.

This is the case of an unfortunate individual who, to hide from a furious husband who unexpectedly burst into the house, clung to the windowsill for at least 5 minutes.

The new acrobat had suffered from chronic lower back pain for almost 20 years. After this interminable period suspended in the void, the pain never reappeared.

The experts who studied the case attributed a miraculous realignment of the facet joints of the lumbar vertebrae to the unexpected prolonged traction, originally lost two decades earlier due to trauma.
