Choosing the Condom

It is strongly recommended to opt for super-lubricated condoms that significantly improve the fluidity of movement. The use of "stimulating" ones is highly discouraged, as any roughness on the external surface of the condom causes a vibratory effect that translates into an incessant bombardment of micro-traumas on the lesion. The use of "delaying" varieties is also not advisable in these circumstances. On the other hand, clinical experience in the field has shown that pain, by its nature, is an exceptional inhibitor and that, in acute phases, the effort to raise and maintain the phallic axis even a few degrees above the horizon line makes any measure to prolong service life counterproductive.

For encounters made in total darkness, "phosphorescent condoms" are absolutely essential. In case of simultaneous acute low back pain, in fact, a series of repeated unsuccessful attempts to hit the target could cause further worsening.
